FERNANDO ALONSO - SPANISH RACING DRIVER

FERNANDO ALONSO is a racing driver who participated in Formula One (F1), World Endurance Championship (WEC), Indycar, and more.

Over his career he won 2x F1 championship, 1x WEC championship. Additonally, he is a 2x winner at 24 Hours of Le Mans, 1x winner of 24 Hours of Daytona.

Alonso has raced in F1 since 2001,

the longest of any driver in F1 history. Alonso has raced with Jordan, Renault, McLaren, Ferrari, Alpine, and Aston Martin
